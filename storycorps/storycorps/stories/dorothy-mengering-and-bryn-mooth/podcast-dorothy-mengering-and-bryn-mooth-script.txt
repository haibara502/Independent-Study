

			
			




			
					Dorothy Mengering died April 12, 2017 at the age of 95. Born in 1921, before there was electricity in her home outside of Linton, Indiana, Dorothy was best known as “Dave’s mom.” She was a regular on her son, David Letterman’s late night television talk shows and covered the 1994 and 1998 Winter Olympics in Lillehammer, Norway, and Nagano, Japan.In this 2007 StoryCorps interview, she tells her granddaughter, Bryn Mooth, about her television experiences.You can read Dorothy’s obituary .
